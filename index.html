About me

With roughly 8 years of cross-sector experience, including 2 years in consulting, 5 years in a DAX-listed corporation, and most recently as a consultant in telecommunications, I have deeply engaged with compliance, risk management, and IT-related projects. Over recent years, I have expanded my competencies in data analysis, completed a certificate program, and acquired proficiency in Python. My aim is to leverage my expertise in roles that prioritize digitalization or operate at its interface.

Key projects I was involved with in Corporate Risk & Compliance Management:

    • Group Integrated Risk Management Tool: Roll-out, implementation, user-trainings, permissions etc.
    • Compliance Management System Reporting with dynamic forms and scoring model
    • International Compliance Culture Survey (annually) and Power BI dashboards for result visualization
    • Compliance KPI-dashboards and reporting with e.g. Power BI
    • Integration of ESG criteria into Executive Board remuneration
    • Digitalization roadmap for Risk & Compliance department
    • Creation of Compliance ticketing system with Power Automate & -Apps
    • Monte Carlo simulations for risk-bearing capacity calculations
    • Self-Assessment documentation with Microsoft Power Apps
    • Creation of process map for Risk & Compliance department with Visio
    • Process automations with Python e.g. for specific data exports
    • Corporate-level Policy Management System: Templates, permissions etc.
    • Administrator of the electronic whistleblower system (BKMS System)
